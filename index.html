Embrace Innovations

Embrace is a social enterprise that advances maternal and child health by delivering an innovative, affordable infant warmer to the world's most vulnerable populations.

Founded out of the Design for Extreme Affodability program at Stanford, Embrace is headquartered in Bangalore India.

I joined Embrace in India as its 7th employee and became responsible for developing our flagship product, an infant warmer that could keep infants warm without requiring electricity while remaining affordable to the poor.

When I left in October 2013 our product had completed preliminary clinical trials, launched into the market in a pilot, and the company had grown to over 50 people.

Connect Four

This was the first web app I ever built and was the outcome of the first project assignment from the General Assembly WDI course of which I was a student.

Originally built in Rails without any javascript, I have since added in javascript and jQuery and page updating via ajax so that players don't have to refresh the page to see new moves.

  • Rails
  • Javascrip
